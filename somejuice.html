Mathematics

Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills

Mathematics in high school covers a wide range of topics that build upon foundational concepts and prepare students for advanced studies and real-world applications.

Key Topics Include:

  • Algebra: Equations, inequalities, functions, and polynomials
  • Geometry: Shapes, theorems, proofs, and spatial reasoning
  • Trigonometry: Trigonometric functions, identities, and applications
  • Calculus: Limits, derivatives, integrals, and their applications
  • Statistics: Data analysis, probability, and statistical inference

Science

Explore the natural world through observation and experimentation

High school science education provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the natural world, scientific methodologies, and the role of science in society.

Key Disciplines:

  • Biology: Cell structure, genetics, evolution, and ecology
  • Chemistry: Atomic structure, chemical reactions, and organic chemistry
  • Physics: Motion, energy, electricity, and magnetism
  • Earth Science: Geology, meteorology, and astronomy
  • Environmental Science: Ecosystems, conservation, and sustainability
